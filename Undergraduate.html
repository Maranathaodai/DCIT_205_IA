Our programs

A Minor in computer science serves as an introductory exploration into both theoretical and applied aspects of computer science, serving as a valuable supplement to your studies in other disciplines. This also grants you the opportunity to enroll in up to three computer science courses at the 300-level or above.

A Major in computer science expands upon the foundation laid by the Minor, gearing you towards advanced studies in computer science with opportunities to delve deeper into specific topics. Those pursuing the Computer Science Major have the flexibility to integrate their studies with another discipline, allowing for a more comprehensive and interdisciplinary educational experience.

A Combined-major in computer science and another field allows students to concurrently study both disciplines in-depth. This integrated program provides a comprehensive understanding of computer science alongside the chosen complementary subject, fostering a well-rounded skill set and a refined perspective that can be applied across various domains.

Our Undergraduate Community

An undergraduate education extends beyond the curriculum it offers. Enrolling in the computer science program at the University of Ghana entails becoming a member of a close-knit community comprised of students, faculty, and alumni who share common interests. Numerous undergraduates express a sense of belonging to a cohesive group. They often complete their program alongside the same students they started with, cultivating a robust network of friendships and study companions throughout the intervening years.
