WELCOME

In the era of Information Technology, Computer Science serves as its fundamental cornerstone. A thorough understanding of computer science is indispensable for society to fully leverage the advantages offered by Information Technology and make swift economic progress. The discipline encompasses the exploration and creation of applications that facilitate our daily interaction with computers, the software systems supporting these applications, and the techniques employed in producing applications and systems that exhibit reliable, correct, and ethical behavior. At the core of Computer Science lies the proficiency in computer programming, and employers seeking Computer Scientists anticipate skilled programmers equipped with knowledge of contemporary techniques and tools, along with the ability to apply sound engineering principles to develop, construct, and maintain computer programs.

The department offers a diverse array of courses, and the acquired skills empower students to pursue employment across various sectors of the economy. Graduates are well-prepared for the computing industry, where they can contribute to project teams or research and development teams, bringing specialized skills to the table. The program also serves as a foundation for further study or research by exposing students to established and emerging technologies and methods, as well as active research areas within the field of Computer Science.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Our Organization

The University of Ghana stands as one of Africa's foremost public universities, exerting a significant influence on individuals, the local community, and the global arena. Whether propelling young minds toward limitless possibilities or tackling pressing issues through bold research, our impact is profound. With an annual enrollment of over 38,000 students, including around 2.64% in the field of computer science, we actively convert ideas into tangible outcomes, shaping the world in which we live.
