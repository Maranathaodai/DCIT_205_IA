Hackathon

The University of Ghana hosts an annual hackathon, fostering innovation and collaboration among students. Participants showcase their coding skills, developing solutions to real-world challenges. The event encourages teamwork and creativity, providing a platform for students to apply theoretical knowledge to practical problem-solving. The hackathon at the University of Ghana promotes a vibrant tech community and prepares students for the dynamic field of computer science.

DevFest

The University of Ghana's DevFest is an annual technology conference that brings together students and industry professionals. This event features insightful talks, workshops, and networking opportunities, promoting skill development and collaboration. DevFest at the university serves as a platform for sharing cutting-edge technologies and fostering a vibrant tech community. It inspires innovation and prepares students for the evolving landscape of the tech industry.
